Brief

Find and understand user concerns in-store at Tiffany & Co., then address them by using advanced technologies to improve in-store user experiences, while also reducing company costs and sustainability issues.








Outcome Summary

Our team of designers created a fully immersive user experience
in-store at Tiffany & Co. By utilizing augmented reality paired with a device
we developed, users can see and feel the weight of jewellery without jewellery
being present in-store. This would provide users with the social experience
they want, and saves Tiffany's millions each year.
