Outcome Summary

I created a mobile app that addresses key ecological mistakes
Chinese students make in the United Kingdom. By understanding their
culture-driven habits, I was able to help them comprehend their recycing
mistakes, prevent food waste and lower waste mismanagement. The app
was designed to teach Chinese students about the recycling system used
in the UK while also catering to their social needs of connecting to others.
