Brief

Identify missed opportunities for ScS to enhance their omni-channel user experiences and redesign the ScS website to provide research-based suggestions that will address key issues for users on their sofa purchasing journey.





















Outcome Summary

Our team of designers found many areas ScS was missing
opportunites to support their users. From social media,
to a inconsistent omni-channel experience, we addressed all areas
that needed attention. While ScS is still working to implement
all these suggestions what they have changed so far has
increased their annual sales by 20%, enhanced their brand equity,
as well as strengthen their relationships with their customers.
Below are our suggestions and how ScS has begun to apply
them to their omni-channel experience.
